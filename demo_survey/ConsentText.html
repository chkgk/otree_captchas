Research Goal:
We are interested in how people fill-in surveys.

Duration:
This study takes about 10 minutes to complete.

Compensation:
Upon successful completion, participants are compensated for their time through the online platform we used to recruit them. For some tasks, there is a chance to earn a bonus payment which will be paid in addition to the lump sum payment for participation.

Data protection and confidentiality:
We either collect data in anonymized form or anonymize the data prior to data analysis. We do not link names or contact details to your answers and choices in this study. The data we collect will be exclusively used for research purposes. Only anonymized data will be shared with other researchers and the public.

Contact:
If you have any questions or concerns before, during, or after participating in the study, please contact the experimenter through the recruitment platform.
